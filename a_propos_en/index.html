About
"My name is Mathieu, I am 21 years old and I chose to work in the audiovisual industry 3 years ago. I am began my journey as a as a video editor in a small company during my OTC on a rotating basis and I discovered that it was for me a real passion. I have extended my skills to photography, I have progressed in the realisation of of special effects, I had the chance to supervise a film crew... on audiovisual projects that are very different from each other... other: short films, corporate films, live mutlicam, commercials for major brands, and television dramas and cinema, in France and abroad. These experiences have made me what I am today, a cameraman, telepilot, editor... I'm versatile and as the projects that I am entrusted with, I focus more and more on the shooting and lighting with the ambition to one day be a director of photography. I like advertising, music videos and the fiction that in my opinion allow the most freedom for executive production. Now, I wish to work with professionals who share the same values as me to produce, together, our most beautiful achievements. »
